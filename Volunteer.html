Volunteer

Serving humanity, at the hour of need

Check out the ways you can help give back by helping us to make a difference in the community.

Volunteers are an integral part of KKM efforts to stomp out hunger and poverty in the United States and around the world. Our volunteers are both children and adults who work directly with the needy and in administrative and planning areas.
We understand that some people’s schedules only allow them to help out occasionally, while others are interested in making the commitment to take on a leadership role. That’s why KKM offers multiple volunteer programs.

Food Distribution Volunteers

Volunteers who help out with our food donation programs gain hands on community service experience as well as the satisfaction of doing their part to stomp out hunger. We have an ongoing need for people to fill the following roles: Food Sorter, Food Distribution Assistance, Special Event Volunteers.
